# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
